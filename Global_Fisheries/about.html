Global Fisheries & Traders is a leading provider of quality seafoods from different regions of India. With processing centres in close proximity to the seashore, Global Fisheries is able to provide the clients products with atmost freshness.

Global Fisheries owns and operates two state of the art procesing centres which is supported by its fleet of fishing vessels. The fresh water farms owned and operated by Global Fisheries breeds mud crabs and tiger prawns mainly for Asia markets. The retail oulet in Kerala is providing seafod for local market.

Global Fisheries within a few span of years is able to grow to the frontier of seafood business with its committed group of employees who have been the back bone for success.
