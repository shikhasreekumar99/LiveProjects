About Nipin Niravath

Nipin Niravath is Kerala's foremost mentalist, magician and psychological entertainer. He is expertise in Neuro-Linguistic Programming(NLP), body language analysing, hypnosis and art of magic. His knowledge in the world of mentalism is abundant, and his dedication toward this art is really great. Since redefining the genre of magic for intelligent, modern audiences, he has become synonymous with the art of psychological manipulation. Niravath has been putting on breathtaking performances for 25 years and has dazzled millions of people across the country. He still holds the record for the youngest magician to perform 'The Great Fire Escape' in india.

Niravath has become increasingly well known for his mind reading show and stage hypnotism perfomance in the "Ugram Ujjwalam", a reality show on television channel Mazhavil Manorama for displaying the breath taking acts and eyes glittering performances of special talented induviduals across the world. "Captivating and fascinating" is how audiences describe Niravath's programs. He still continues his perfomance on different TV shows as “special guest performer” and displays his skills of real mind powers and mind controls. With a number of stage shows and TV appearances Niravath continues to amaze audiences with his unique blend of magic, mind reading and motivational programs.

Niravath has a fascinating history. The most exciting fact about Niravath is that there wasn't any teacher in his magical life and what ever he performs is his own ideas and innovations. His hobbies include balloon twisting, shadowgraphy, psychological magic perfomances, mind hacking etc.He also finds time in his busy schedule to cherish the lonely heart of intellectually challenged and orphan people with out any rewards. He now lives in Kochi, Kerala with his family.
