HOBBIES

Balloon twisting

Nipin Niravath is purely a gifted person in the art of balloon twisting. He is the most talented and entertaining balloon sculpting artist in Kochi today who is able to create an extensive range of exquisite balloon sculptures. Balloon sculpting entertainment service is a hot favorite at birthday parties or events as every child will get to take his or her very own amazing balloon sculpture home. Niravath is a specialist in shaping of special modelling balloons into almost any given shape, from animals to vehicles to cartoon parodies. From private parties to big events, he aim to achieve the finest artwork in every decoration and sculpting services, by using good quality materials and the very best balloons made in the world. Explore the world of balloon art created by the leading balloon twister in Kerala.

Shadowgraphy

Shadowgraphy a unique art form which is a combination of hands and fingers on a blank screen telling you a million stories with complete audio visual effect. This art form has immense possibilities and can be applied in different fields. Nipin Niravath is one of such artist known for his excellence in an amazing array of fields. He is blessed with a rare talent to produce a world of light and shade, presenting images and stories in a narrative structure with just bare hands and hold audience of all age groups completely mesmerized. Explore the reaches of his imagination, watch him amaze the audience and be amazed as well.
