About Verghmens Electricals

We would like to introduce ourselves as a CPRI certified Medium Voltage Electrical Panel Board manufacturer having our factory situated at Ezhakkaranadu(S), Puthencruze in Ernakulam district, Kerala.


Running Since 1990

Our clients include BPCL, Kochi Refinery, Ambalamugal; Kerala Minerals and Metals Ltd., Kollam; The Fertilizers and Chemical Travancore Ltd, Udyogamandal & Ambalamedu; Nitta Gelatin India Ltd (Formerly Kerala Chemicals and Proteins Ltd.) Kakkanadu; Indian Rare Earths Limited, Udyogamandal; Kerala State Drugs and Pharmaceuticals Ltd. Alappuzha; Medical college Thiruvananthapuram (M/s. RDS Projects); Paarippilly ESIC Medical College (M/s. L&T); Kerala Water Authority (M/s. GNX Engineers), LuLu International Shopping Mall, Edappally etc.

Our factory is equipped with all the required machinery for manufacturing the products mentioned. These machinery includes Hydraulic Shearing machine, Hydraulic Bending machine, Power press, Fly Press, Pedestal Drilling machines, Compressor, Taping machine, Welding sets, Grinders etc. We can supply panels powder coated as per clients requirement.

We have with us the testing facilities to test the equipments manufactured by us. The equipments include Tong tester, CT Polarity tester, Phase Sequence Meter, HV Testing Kit, Megger, Multimeter etc. Our panels have been tested at Central Power Research Institute, Bangalore for Short circuit current of 50KA, Temperature rise test and Power frequency withstand test.

We have in our employment highly skilled technical staff to undertake the best quality electrical fabrication work.
