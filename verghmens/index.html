About
Verghmens Electricals

Our Service

Retrofitting

Our Retrofit service is innovatively designed for renovating existing electrical systems, minimizing demolition of existing walls and eventual repair costs when upgrading electrical distribution systems in older facilities.

Our primary activities are the design and manufacture of quality made & competitively priced Medium Voltage Electrical Panel Boards and the provision of Electrical Systems Consultancy for a global client base.

Verghmens Retrofit Electric division is a full service electrical manufacturer. With more than 25 years in the industry, our crews consistently rate high in customer satisfaction.
